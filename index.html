Happy Birthday, Dad! 🎂
This game is inspired by you and your game of 11 . This is a little daily math challenge to keep the brain sharp!

How to play:

  • Each day has 3 levels of increasing difficulty.
  • Your goal: combine all the given numbers using +, -, *, / to reach exactly 11.
  • Use each number exactly once.
  • All intermediate steps must stay integers (no fractions or decimals).
  • If your answer is incorrect, you’ll get feedback “No, try again!” — keep experimenting!

Good luck and have fun! 🧠✨
